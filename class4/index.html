Waffle Wednesdays

For the love of waffles! And a little tech networking too.

#WaffleWednesday is a weekly breakfast networking event for Miami's tech and creative community to come together. Each week we offer up gourmet waffle creations, bulletproof coffee, fruit, and Caribe Juice. Come introduce yourself to the community and get the chance to hear from local startups and businesses as they pitch their app, demo new projects, and share upcoming events with the room.

Invite your friends. Bring your mom. Come kickstart your day with us at Waffle Wednesday!
